Universe

Type: Virtual reality, Interactive experience, Personal project

Description: This is a VR-based cosmic interactive project. It aims to introduce the scientific knowledge of the evolution of the universe, from the Big Bang to the formation of earth and moon. Through this interactive project, users can experience a series of important astronomical events, such as the Big Bang, accretion, supernova synthesis, the giant impact, etc., in an immersive and interactive way.

Tools: Unity3D, HTC Vive
