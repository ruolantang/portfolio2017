Cololing

Type: Video game, Racing, Color detection, Personal project

Description: An indie game that combines the gameplay of color detecting with racing. The ball that is moving forward will occasionally change its color. The runway features four columns of very similar colors. You need to identify the column that matches the color of the ball and move the ball to the corresponding track to knock the column down.

Tools: Unity3D


Click here to play.

Please make sure that your browser supports WebGL.

Instruction: Press the left/right arrow key to move the ball. Try to find the one with the different color and knock it down!
