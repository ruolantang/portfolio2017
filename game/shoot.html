Shoot the music

Type: Music game

Description: A game that combines the gameplay of shooting with music. Musical notes fall from the sky, and you need to shoot them according to the rhythm of the song. We convert the audio files into MIDI forms and then extract rhythms out of the data.

My contributions: Game design, gameplay programming

Tools: Unity3D, Cubase 7
